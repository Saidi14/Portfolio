    Our Mission

    Our Mission from God ties in with the Great Commission – To take the Light of the Truth of God’s Word to preach, teach and demonstrate it wherever we go in the World and in the Body of Christ. Our Mission is based on the scripture Isaiah 61:1-3

    Light Minstries International

    Our Values

    We are committed to Prayer and Fasting. We give high emphasis to Prayer and Fasting. We walk by Faith in God’s Word. We yield totally to the Holy Spirit and the Holy Spirit is in charge in all we do. We reach out, teach and equip others for the work of the ministry. We network with other Ministries and stay connected in the Body of Christ.

    About the ministry

    Light Ministries International is based in Rugby Milnerton, Cape Town, South Africa. It is founded on biblical principles and values. The ministry is also twofold, meaning that we both minister in the church, as well as outside the church to prostitutes, the homeless and people from all walks of life inviting people to come to church and empowering ourselves to go out and be the church. ...

    • We get together on Wednesday, for cottage meetings/ homesells/ connect groups where we get together for prayer, fellowship and swallowship (having a cup of coffee and a light meal).
    • Thursday mornings at 10, we do prayer and intercession, and Friday evenings, the youth get together.
    • We do random feedings for people living on the street. In our church, every Sunday, we have a benevolent, or a love box where we collect tins of food and then we bless a family in the church. We are blessed to be a blessing.

    We also do feeding weekly at 62 Justin Street, focusing on the school children. We give them a plate of food or a cup of soup and bread. We also have a soup kitchen that runs in Albow Gardens, where we feed the community. Our motto is We cannot do everything, but we can do something.
